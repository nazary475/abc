## DETAILED DESCRIPTION OF MAIN ACTIVITY

### I. LEGAL NATURE AND CORPORATE PURPOSE

Haal Lab is a company specializing in the design, development, and deployment of artificial intelligence systems adapted to the specific constraints of European organizations. The main activity focuses on solving two major challenges faced by European businesses, administrations, and institutions wishing to benefit from artificial intelligence:

**First challenge: Data sovereignty and regulatory compliance**
European regulations (GDPR, NIS2 Directive, EU AI Act) impose strict constraints on data processing and circulation. Artificial intelligence solutions hosted on non-European infrastructure present unacceptable legal and operational risks for many organizations. Haal Lab builds AI systems operating entirely on the client's infrastructure or on certified European infrastructure, ensuring that data never leaves European jurisdiction.

**Second challenge: Reliability and operational control**
Artificial intelligence systems must operate in a predictable, verifiable, and maintainable manner in critical production environments. "Black box" solutions whose functioning cannot be understood do not meet the operational and regulatory requirements of responsible organizations. Haal Lab delivers AI systems with complete observability, continuous evaluation mechanisms, and documentation enabling internal teams to understand, operate, and improve systems autonomously.

### II. AREAS OF EXPERTISE AND SERVICES PROVIDED

To address these two European challenges, Haal Lab offers five complementary areas of expertise:

**A. SOVEREIGN AND COMPLIANT AI SYSTEMS**

Design and implementation of artificial intelligence solutions operating entirely on the client's infrastructure (servers in Europe, certified data centers, or isolated environments). This approach guarantees GDPR compliance by design: sensitive data never circulates to non-European jurisdictions, eliminating legal risks related to international data transfers.

Systems are optimized to run on the client's existing infrastructure, whether modest servers or advanced computing clusters. The artificial intelligence models used are open-weight, allowing independent verification, adaptation to specific needs, and complete independence from foreign commercial vendors.

Services include:
- Architecture and deployment of AI systems on certified European infrastructure
- GDPR, EU AI Act, and sector-specific regulatory compliance (health, finance, defense)
- Configuration of systems isolated from public networks for highly secure environments
- Optimization for existing hardware without requiring massive infrastructure investments
- Legal and technical documentation for compliance audits
- Training of internal teams for autonomous system operation

**B. INTELLIGENT APPLICATIONS**

Development of artificial intelligence applications capable of processing, understanding, and generating content. These applications go beyond simple conversational interfaces: they understand business context, adapt to specialized terminologies of each sector, and can be customized according to the organization's specific needs.

Developed applications include virtual assistants for customer support, automatic document analysis systems, workflow automation, and intelligent agents capable of orchestrating actions on existing information systems.

Each application integrates quality control mechanisms, human validation points when necessary, and complete decision traceability from the design phase to meet EU AI Act transparency and explainability requirements.

Services include:
- Design of virtual assistants adapted to the organization's business needs
- Automatic document processing systems
- Business workflow automation
- Integration with existing information systems and databases
- User interfaces adapted to business contexts
- Validation, traceability, and explainability mechanisms for automated decisions

**C. INTELLIGENT SEARCH IN DOCUMENTS AND KNOWLEDGE**

Construction of systems enabling instant retrieval of relevant information in vast document corpora. These systems address a critical need for European organizations: effectively exploiting their documentary heritage (contracts, reports, scientific publications, technical documentation, archives) often dispersed across multiple formats.

The system combines keyword-based and meaning-based similarity search to identify relevant passages, then intelligently ranks results by relevance. Each answer provided is accompanied by its exact sources (document, page, section), enabling human verification and compliance with explainability requirements.

For complex documents (scanned legal contracts, scientific publications with tables and charts, technical documents with diagrams), we integrate optical character recognition technologies and document structure analysis.

Services include:
- Intelligent search systems in technical, legal, or scientific documentation
- Document processing respecting specificities of each format
- Automatic information extraction from structured and unstructured documents
- Precise source attribution for each extracted piece of information
- Processing of varied formats: scanned PDFs, office documents, web pages, emails
- Indexing and search in historical archives

**D. RELIABLE OPERATING PLATFORM**

Construction of infrastructure enabling artificial intelligence systems to operate reliably, predictably, and maintainably in production environments. This infrastructure includes optimized model deployment, real-time performance monitoring, automatic anomaly detection, and mechanisms for continuously improving quality.

The objective is to provide organizations with an AI platform they can operate autonomously, understand, monitor, and evolve without depending on rare skills or permanent external support. Documentation, monitoring dashboards, and operational procedures are integral parts of the deliverable.

Services include:
- Deployment of AI systems on European infrastructure (cloud or private servers)
- Configuration of automatic monitoring: alerts, performance metrics, anomaly detection
- Optimization of operating costs and system performance
- Automatic adaptation mechanisms for load variations
- Complete operational documentation and internal team training
- Maintenance, update, and continuous improvement procedures

**E. SPECIALIZED MODEL TRAINING**

Development of specialized artificial intelligence models trained specifically for precise business tasks. Rather than adapting massive generic models, we build compact, optimized models trained from the ground up for a particular task, offering better performance with significantly reduced infrastructure requirements.

This approach enables the creation of efficient, fast, and resource-economical AI systems that run on modest infrastructure while achieving excellent performance in their specialized domain. Each model is designed with the architecture best suited to the task: document classification, information extraction, sentiment analysis, or other specific needs.

This specialization is particularly relevant for business domains with specific technical vocabularies (law, medicine, finance, engineering) where a compact dedicated model often outperforms bulky generic models.

Services include:
- Selection of optimal architecture according to the specific task
- Training of classification models for sector document categorization
- Development of information extraction models on proprietary terminology
- Creation of analysis models adapted to specific business needs
- Constitution of high-quality annotated training datasets
- Optimization for operation on lightweight infrastructure
- Continuous improvement with new examples over time
- Comparative evaluation demonstrating achieved performance gains
- Complete ownership: client owns trained models and data

### III. ENGAGEMENT METHODOLOGY

Our approach follows a structured four-phase process:

**Phase 1: Discovery (1-2 weeks)**
In-depth understanding of business problem, technical and regulatory constraints, data availability, success criteria, and target production environment.

**Phase 2: Architecture (2-3 weeks)**
Complete system design including model choices, processing strategy, infrastructure architecture, and evaluation methodology. Validation against real workloads via targeted prototypes before full development commitment.

**Phase 3: Development (variable by scope)**
Engineering in short, demonstrable increments. Client sees working software regularly, accompanied by evaluation reports on metrics defined in Phase 1.

**Phase 4: Deployment and Transfer**
Deployment into target environment with observability, operational documentation, runbooks, and training necessary for autonomous operation by client team. Post-deployment support to ensure stability.

### V. GUIDING PRINCIPLES

Four architectural principles guide every technical decision:

**1. Sovereignty by Design**
Data sovereignty is an architectural property of the system, not a policy document. Models run on client infrastructure when it matters, eliminating risks by design.

**2. Applied Research**
Continuous monitoring of AI advances (retrieval architectures, acceleration, evaluation methodologies) and translation into operational systems once production maturity is reached.

**3. Rigorous Engineering**
Observability, evaluation, and reproducibility are integral parts of the deliverable. Systems must be operable and understandable, not hidden black boxes.

**4. Open by Default**
Use of open-weight models and open-source infrastructure. Client owns the system, models, and data without any technological lock-in.
